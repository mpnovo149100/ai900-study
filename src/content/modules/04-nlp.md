# Natural Language Processing (NLP)

> Replace this starter content with your own Word summary for this module.

## Text capabilities (Azure AI Language)

- **Sentiment analysis** — positive / negative / neutral + confidence scores.
- **Key phrase extraction** — the main points in a text.
- **Entity recognition** — people, places, organisations, dates.
- **Language detection** — identify the language of the text.
- **Question answering & CLU** — build conversational understanding.

## Speech capabilities (Azure AI Speech)

- **Speech-to-text** — transcription (real-time or batch).
- **Text-to-speech** — natural neural voices.
- **Translation** — speech and text across languages.
- **Custom Speech** — adapt to domain vocabulary, accents, and noisy audio.
