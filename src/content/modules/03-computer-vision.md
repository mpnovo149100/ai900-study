# Computer Vision

> Replace this starter content with your own Word summary for this module.

## Capabilities

- **Image classification** — assign a label to a whole image.
- **Object detection** — locate objects with bounding boxes.
- **OCR** — extract printed/handwritten text from images and PDFs.
- **Image analysis** — tags, captions, objects, and metadata.
- **Facial detection/recognition** — detect and identify faces.

## Azure services

- **Azure AI Vision** — pre-built image analysis, OCR, spatial analysis.
- **Custom Vision** — train domain-specific classification/detection models
  with relatively few images.
- **Azure AI Face** — face detection and recognition.

The Vision SDK's `analyze()` accepts **multiple visual features in one call**
(e.g. `OBJECTS`, `READ`, `CAPTION`).
